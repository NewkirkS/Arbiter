Arbiter

Insert URL of choice into the Arbiter to begin analysis based on emotional tones found in content. Common subjects include: news articles, song lyrics, and social media (the more text the better).

Arbiter generates confidence scores for 5 emotions (anger, disgust, fear, joy, and sadness). Scores represent the probabilty by percentage that the corrensponding emotion is implied in text.

Using the Alchemy API, the Arbiter uses machine learning algorithms to detect emotions in words, phrases, punctuation, and overall sentiments from provided source.
